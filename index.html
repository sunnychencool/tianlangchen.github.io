About Me

I am a fifth-year PhD student in the Computer Science Department, University of Rochester, advised by Prof. Jiebo Luo. My current research interests lie in Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing and Deep Learning. I have spent time at Google Research, Adobe Research, Bytedance AI Lab U.S. and Tencent Youtu Lab as a research intern during my Ph.D. career. Prior to this, I obtained my bachelor degree from Electronic and Information Engineering, University of Science and Technology of China, supervised by Prof. Xinmei Tian.
